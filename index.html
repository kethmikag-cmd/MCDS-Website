About Us

Who Are We?

The Media Circle of D. S. Senanayake College, better known as MCDSSC and spanning a history of more than three decades since inauguration, is a trilingual school club recognized across Sri Lanka for its efforts to nurture and inspire the next generation of pioneers in the Sri Lankan Media genealogy.

Vision

To become a leading media unit in Sri Lanka and assuring a standard place for D. S. Senanayake College in the Sri Lankan school announcing arena while marking each member a successful media personality in the country.

Mission

To promote and develop the vocal skills and announcing skills among the school students as an important cocurricular activity to produce media personalities with a proper awareness and development in media affiliated skills.

History

Starting off from humble beginnings in 1994, the rise to prominence and prestige of Media Circle of D. S. Senanayake College is a continuous decades-long tale of unwavering commitment, dedication and vision to conquer its place as the flagbearers of Sri Lanka's evolving media lineage.

Publications

Siyapatha Magazine

Siyapatha is the only trillingual school magazine by D.S.Senanayake College, published as three editions over three consecutive academic terms every year. Released officially in digital and paperback format, the magazine is a treasure trove of the diverse ideas, artistic creativity, and vibrant talent of the Senanayakian community.


The magazine collects diverse forms of writing like poetry, short stories, articles and opinion pieces (except radically political content) in all three main languages: Sinhala, English, Tamil. There are also sections dedicated for written interviews of school officials, teachers, and prominent Senanayakian alumni including reflections on their involvement and time at D.S.Senananayake College, event briefings and also creative art pieces by Senanayakians.


More than the mere objective of improving talent of young Senanayakians and encouraging reading, Siyapatha upholds the cultural continuity of D.S.Senanayake College.

What is Ninnada'26?

Ninnada is the trademark event of the Media Circle of D.S.Senanayake College. It is the largest and most prestigious all-island trillingual media competition and day in Sri Lanka, and its well-recognized for representing the most talented young media enthusiasts across the country. The judging panel will feature renowned media personalities to ensure a high-quality competitive experience and fair evaluations. The overall champions of Ninnada will receive the Hon. D.S. Senanayake Memorial Shield, while the Best School Student Media Personality will be awarded the prestigious R.I.T. Alles Memorial Trophy.
